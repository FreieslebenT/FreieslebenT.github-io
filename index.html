Webpage of Timo Freiesleben

Hi visitor, nice to have you here on my website. I am a researcher at the Machine Learning in Science Cluster at the University of Tübingen with Dr. Thomas Grote. My research centers around:

  • Interpretable Machine Learning or Explainable AI
  • Philosophy of Science and Causality
  • Ethics of AI and Algorithmic Fairness
  • Philosophy and Theory of Machine Learning

I have recently submitted my PhD thesis to LMU Munich entitled: ''What Does Explainable AI Explain?'' which I will defend in the next few weeks. My PhD was supervised by Prof. Stephan Hartmann and Dr. Alvaro Tejero-Cantero and was based at the Munich Center for Mathematical Philosophy and the Graduate School of Systemic Neurosciences.

Prior to my PhD, I studied philosophy of science, also at MCMP, and mathematics at the University of Tübingen and the University of Oslo. My complete CV can be found here (Version 12-2022).
